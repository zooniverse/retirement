Ancient Lives

Ancient lives is a project that asks volunteers to transcribe ancient Greek text on fragments from the Oxyrhynchus Papyri collection. It is currently being rebuilt, and the new site will be live very soon! Meanwhile, try some of our other projects or read existing publications based on the data.
